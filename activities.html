Activities and Events

Always lots to do and see in the area! Hiking, biking, skiing, fishing, mountain climbing, wildlife viewing, grabbing up that super bargain during the flea markets or just plain relaxing by the campfire. For a list of scheduled activities visit Destination Packwood.

Lakes and rivers abound for fishing, swimming and rafting. A variety of hunting and snow sports available seasonally includes miles of snowmobile trails. Horseback riding, golfing and a swimming pool are just a few miles away. Wild flower, huckleberry and mushroom picking in the summer while gazing at the amazing wildlife are part of this fantastic countryside.

There are events and activities that continue throughout the year including arts festivals, music, town picnics and barbeques. Motorcycle and auto cross competitions, antique car shows.
